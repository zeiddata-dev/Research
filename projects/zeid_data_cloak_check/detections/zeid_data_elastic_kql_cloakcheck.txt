1) Redirect responses:
http.response.status_code: (301 or 302 or 303 or 307 or 308)

2) Multi-hop redirect chains (visualize by session/user + url):
http.response.status_code: (301 or 302 or 303 or 307 or 308) and url.original: *

3) Rare domains (requires aggregation in Lens):
destination.domain: *  (then aggregate by destination.domain and filter low doc_count and low unique users)

4) Content length drift (if you have http.response.body.bytes):
http.response.body.bytes: * and url.original: *
(then aggregate min/max bytes by url.original)
